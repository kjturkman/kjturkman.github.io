What Is a Bistro?

A bistro serves gourmet-quality food quickly at at good value. We do this by specializing in slow-cooked dishes, prepared fresh each morning and ready to serve quickly throughout the day.
As for the word bistro, legend says it came from Russian soldiers in 19th cent. Paris who wanted to be served bistro (Russian for quickly).

Menu

Wholesome

It all starts with fresh seasonal ingredients that pack as much nutrition as they do flavor. We strive to make food that is both good and good for you.

Fusion

We love to experiment with new and exciting combinations of flavors, using ingredients from all over the world but especially our native countries of Poland and Malaysia.

Halal

All our meat is certified halal and of the highest quality. This means you can be sure it is hormone-free, antibiotic-free, and ethically raised. We use no pork or alcohol in our kitchen.
